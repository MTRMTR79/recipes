Vannila Ice cream


Description

People always think Vannila is the plain flavor, for when there isn't really a flavor. Ohhh boy are they wrong. This recipe proves that. This is an amazing recipe I have now used many times to get delecate Vannila flavor.


Ingredients

  • ¼ cup sugar
  • 1 cup cream
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 vannila bean
  • A pinch of salt
  • 7 egg yolks

Steps

  1. In a medium pot over medium heat, add sugar, cream, milk, salt, and the almighty vannila bean. Heat until sugar is disolved, and it is almost to a boil, but not quite. Turn off heat and let it steep for 30 minutes to an hour (or longer if you want more flavour).
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk yolks. Whisking constantly, slowly whisk about a third of the hot cream into the yolks, then whisk the yolk mixture back into the pot with the cream.
  3. Return pot to medium-low heat and gently cook until mixture is thick enough to coat the back of a spoon.
  4. Strain through a fine-mesh sieve into a bowl, . Cool mixture to room temperature. Cover and chill at least 4 hours or overnight.
  5. Churn in an ice cream machine according to manufacturer’s instructions. Serve directly from the machine for soft serve, or store in freezer until needed.
