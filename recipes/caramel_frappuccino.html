Caramel Frappuccino

Recipe from https://www.joshuaweissman.com/post/starbucks-drinks-but-better



Description

A cool, caffinated, sweet drink. The perfect presentation. What more could you want?


Ingredients

  • ½ cup esspresso
  • ¾ cup milk
  • ½ tsp xantham gum
  • 4-6 Tbsp Caramel sauce
  • 2 cups ice

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients into a blender, and blend until the right consistancy for you.
  2. For the perfect presentation, add swirls of caramel sauce to the sides of the glass, pour drink, top with whipped cream, and drizzle caramel sauce on top. Or be lazy and just pour and Drink.
  3. Enjoy!
