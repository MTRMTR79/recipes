Caramel Ice cream

Recipe and image from https://cooking.nytimes.com/recipes/1016626-salted-caramel-ice-cream



Description

This is an amazing recipe I have now used many times to get a luscious caramel flavor with the slight burnt flavor, yet still delicious.


Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 cups cream
  • 1 cup milk
  • A pinch of salt
  • 7 egg yolks

Steps

  1. In a medium pot over medium heat, melt ¾ cup sugar with 3 tablespoons water, swirling skillet frequently, until sugar turns to a liquid and all the water is boiled away.
  2. Slowly add heavy cream and milk while stirring quickly to mix in the caramel and keep the temp high. Once smooth, add remaining ¼ cup sugar and the salt. Once everything is dissolved, remove from heat.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk yolks. Whisking constantly, slowly whisk about a third of the hot cream into the yolks, then whisk the yolk mixture back into the pot with the cream.
  4. Return pot to medium-low heat and gently cook until mixture is thick enough to coat the back of a spoon.
  5. Strain through a fine-mesh sieve into a bowl. Cool mixture to room temperature. Cover and chill at least 4 hours or overnight.
  6. Churn in an ice cream machine according to manufacturer’s instructions. Serve directly from the machine for soft serve, or store in freezer until needed.
